BTR Health – Marketing Plan

Building brand trust through stories, strategy, and care

🧭 Marketing Launch Summary

  • Website & Google SEO: Optimize website for key search terms and enhance Google Business presence with updates and reviews.
  • Social Media (Organic + Paid): Active posting and advertising across platforms to build trust and reach.
  • Strategic Partnerships: Build relationships with local churches (Through Faith stream) and NDIS coordinators/referrers.
  • Flyers & Letterbox Drops: Local physical outreach to boost visibility.

🎯 Marketing Objectives

  • ✅ 10,000 social media followers across 5 platforms
  • ✅ Onboard 12 NDIS participants in Melbourne Southeast
  • ✅ Achieve $597,800 revenue with 30% net margin in Year 1

🎥 Interactive Client Case Studies

We will create short videos showcasing real participant journeys to humanize our brand and build trust.

🌈 Tom’s Supported Getaway

Tom always dreamed of a holiday to his homeland in Vietnam to visit family, but he never thought it was possible—until he discovered Supported Holidays (NDIS-supported travel).

✝️ Beshoy & Ora’s Temple Return

Beshoy and Ora loved going to temple before serious mental disabilities halted visits—after years away, they reconnected with their faith with support worker Rashaun’s help.

💪 Beshoy’s Gym Comeback

After severe calorie counting left him with physical disabilities, Beshoy thought gym days were over—until a support service helped him back to the gym he loves.

🎉 Mukesh’s Night Out

Mukesh loved the party life until an illness at Orchard Towers changed everything—an inclusive nightclub event with support workers let him dance again as he always did.

📣 Marketing Strategy Breakdown

  • Instagram/Facebook: 2 posts & 1 story per week
  • TikTok: 2 short videos per week (NDIS tips, team intros)
  • LinkedIn: Professional NDIS updates & partnerships
  • YouTube: Monthly in-depth story/case study videos
  • Ads: $500–$1,000/month across Meta and Google Ads
  • SEO keywords like “NDIS provider Melbourne Southeast”
  • Update Google Business with reviews, service tags
  • Use virtual addresses to expand reach into Greater Melbourne
  • 2 blogs/month (NDIS tips, guides, spiritual care)
  • Free PDF downloads for email signups
  • Bi-weekly newsletters segmented by audience type
  • Church partnerships with presentations/workshops
  • Attend expos and sponsor inclusive local events
  • Join Facebook groups to offer advice and trust—not sales
  • Letterbox drops, posters in churches & community halls
  • Billboards once budget allows
  • Inspirational QR-code stickers in public spaces
  • Branded merchandise at events (pens, totes)

📈 How We'll Track Success

  • 📊 Social: Followers, likes, comments, click-throughs
  • 🧲 Leads: Email signups, form submissions, ad inquiries
  • 💵 Revenue: Monthly vs $597,800 target
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Participants: Onboarding toward 12 target
